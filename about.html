About ISPOR-HEOR UNN

Learn about our society, mission, committees, leadership, and awards.

Our Society

ISPOR is a dynamic and global organization focused on health economics and outcomes research (HEOR).

Core Mission: Advance pharmacoeconomics for better healthcare decisions. Pharmacoeconomics helps us figure out which medication gives the best result for the best price, ensuring no life is left behind because of cost.

How We Function

  • Campus-based chapter under ISPOR global
  • 7 student-driven committees
  • Common goal: Healthcare excellence

Our Impact

  • Knowledge sharing (conferences, seminars, workshops)
  • Skill building (hands-on training, research trainings)
  • Community engagement (outreaches)
  • Volunteerism & leadership (projects, self-development)

Who We Are: Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

ISPOR's mission is to advance health economics and outcomes research excellence to improve decision making for health globally.

Vision

ISPOR's vision is for a world where healthcare is accessible, effective, efficient, and affordable for all.

Values

Transformative ¬∑ Scientific ¬∑ Inclusive ¬∑ Collaborative ¬∑ Transparent ¬∑ Ethical

Our Members

ISPOR-HEOR UNN is home to a vibrant community of over 500 active members, passionate, forward-thinking students dedicated to advancing healthcare through research, innovation, and impact.

Our members represent diverse interests across pharmacy, medicine, public health, and related disciplines, yet we're united by a common goal: to improve healthcare decision-making through evidence, data, and human-centered insights.

Within our chapter, members don't just learn ‚Äî they lead. From health policy discussions and research projects to community outreaches and professional development programs, ISPOR-HEOR UNN members are actively shaping the future of healthcare.

Our Impact

At ISPOR-HEOR UNN, impact is at the heart of everything we do. Through our outreach programs, research initiatives, and health awareness campaigns, we've reached over 20 communities and schools, touching the lives of more than 10,000 people across different regions.

From promoting health education and preventive care to advancing conversations around healthcare access, our members continue to bridge the gap between knowledge and real-world change.

Each outreach, seminar, and community project is a step toward a healthier society ‚Äî where informed decisions, compassion, and science work hand in hand to improve lives.

ISPOR-HEOR UNN isn't just a student chapter; it's a movement of young professionals driving measurable impact in healthcare, one community at a time.
