FAQs

What is ISPOR-HEOR?

ISPOR-HEOR, formerly The International Society for Pharmacoeconomics and Outcomes Research, is a global health organization dedicated to improving healthcare decision making through health economics and outcomes research.

What is the mission of ISPOR-HEOR?

ISPOR's mission is to advance health economics and outcomes research excellence to improve decision making for health globally. We believe that every healthcare decision should be informed by the best scientific research.

What activities does the University of Nigeria Nsukka's branch of ISPOR-HEOR engage in?

The University of Nigeria Nsukka's branch engages in activities such as seminars, field trips, public health campaigns, medical outreaches, and intensive hands-on training on research to improve healthcare decisions.

How does ISPOR-HEOR contribute to the field of health economics and outcomes research?

ISPOR-HEOR is the leading source for scientific conferences, peer-reviewed and MEDLINE®️-indexed publications, good practices guidance, education, collaboration, and tools/resources in the HEOR field.

How can I stay updated with ISPOR-HEOR's activities and events?

Follow us on our official website, social media channels, or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated with our activities and events.
